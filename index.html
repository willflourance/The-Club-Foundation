About

The Club Foundation was created in 1988 as the only 501(c)3 organization focused solely on the club industry. The Foundation seeks to fund the life cycle of a club manager’s career. Therefore, The Club Foundation provides dollars for the following five key areas: 1) Students 2) Faculty 3) Club Managers 4) CMAA Chapters 5) Industry at Large. The Club Foundation has a donor base of constituents that seek to further the knowledge and skills of those individuals that lead country clubs, yacht clubs, city clubs, and more.

The Club Foundation Auction 2020

Boots, Bourbon and Bling

Boot scoot on over and support The Club Foundation Auction in conjunction with CMAA’s World Conference and Club Business EXPO 2020. This year’s theme is Boots, Bourbon and Bling and we will gratefully accept your donation. This, of course, is in addition to the wine component of the auction that we all know and love.

We’d like to ask each vendor to please consider donating a pair of boots, a piece of bling (cowboy hat, belt buckle or jewelry) or a bottle of Bourbon to the auction. And, just like last year, we’d ask that you purchase the boots from Zappos. Many of last year’s Boot Auction winners elected to exchange their boots for a different size, so when you purchase the boots, please provide us with the following information from Zappos:

Name of purchaser
Email address of purchaser
Billing address of purchaser
Order Confirmation number

All this information can be detailed on the attached donation form and will make exchanges a breeze! Last year’s auction was a huge success with almost 50 pairs of boots sold and more than $15,000 raised in support of The Club Foundation’s grants and scholarship program. This year, we hope to do more! Questions? Please contact Carrie Wosicki at (703) 299-4283 or carrie.wosicki@cmaa.org.
